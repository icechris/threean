Privacy Policy

Payment

Individual clients are kindly requested to make payment in full on the last day of the trip. For companies or organizations, payments are expected within 2 weeks after submission of service invoices. Payment should be made through bank transfer, Mobile Money or cash deposit to our Ecobank Account which is indicated on the invoice

Booking

Booking can be made through our website www.threeancompany.com or by calling or WhatsApp to our office line 0266758586. We encourage you to provide the accurate information during booking to enable us serve you well. After booking an email communication will be made through the clients email and the details of the trip communicated back to you. Please note that, an assigned driver will contact you at least a day before the trip begins.

Fuel

Our vehicles comes with full tank before departure. Clients are expected to for top up fuels whilst on trip. At the end of the trip client will fill the fuel tank at the destination of the car. Since we are based in Kumasi, clients will pay additional fuel when the final destination of the client is outside Kumasi. Please Note: Do not give money to the driver to buy the fuel. Clients are kindly requested to buy the fuel whilst in the car.

Driver

We do not encourage self-drive due to vehicle security features, so our cars comes with experience drivers who know most of the route and places in Ghana. You are kindly requested to discuss your route with him prior to departure to enable him map the rout and avoid traffic. Drivers allowance is paid to him each day for the number of days you will spend with you. The allowance with be communicated to clients during booking.

Insurance

Our cars are comprehensively insured with Hollard insurance. In case of unlikely events, clients, drivers and the vehicle is fully covered in our insurance scheme.

Technical breakdown

Our vehicles are well maintained and serviced. In case of technical breakdown, the driver will liaise with management to fix the problem on the trip. Where management is not convinced third-party mechanic, a new vehicle will be sent from our office in Kumasi to release the driver and the vehicle will be brought back to office for maintenance at no cost to the client.

Security

Our cars are fixed with multiple security features to control speed, location and unlikely situation. In case of stealing of the vehicle, our driver will notify the office immediately and the vehicle will be tracked from office.

Cancellation

Booking can be cancelled latest 1 day to the trip. In case of cancellation, kindly call or WhatsApp to our office line 2066758586 or send email to threeancompany@gmail.com Where cancellation is made one hour to the trip, client will be charged for the day's vehicle cost.

Data protect

Client information is periodically downloaded from the serve and kept on an external drive for safe keeping. This information is not shared with third parties unless its requested by clients
