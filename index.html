About Us

3AN car rentals is a company incorporated in Ghana which provide reliable vehicles for all your travels within Ghana. We are rental company with four years of experience in car rental business focusing on serving a myriad of clients from individuals to corporates. We are experience with dedicated personnel who respond to the needs of client. We aspire to provide current model vehicles in excellent condition both visually and mechanically with all vehicles in our current fleet equipped with radio / CD, fully air-conditioned and seat belts.

Our Vision

we aspire to be the leading car rental company providing excellence services to range of customers in a safe and comforting manner
Our Mission

Is to provide quality transport services using current and well maintained vehicles, experienced and committed staff to our clients.

Our Services

3AN car rental provide a wide range of transportation services across the length and breath of Ghana. Our services include.

  1. #1 Heading for service

    We provide luxurious Toyota land cruiser vehicles for clients such as NGOs or individuals for a short period of time embarking on field trips within Ghana. This comes with an experience dedicated driver, free Wi-Fi, and 500ml bottled water for guest

  2. #2 Heading for service

    We provide mini-van or coaster bus services to organizations, groups or individuals embarking on trips, business, pleasure or ceremonial activities within Ghana

  3. #3 Heading for service

    We provide luxurious vehicles for weddings, funerals, meetings.
